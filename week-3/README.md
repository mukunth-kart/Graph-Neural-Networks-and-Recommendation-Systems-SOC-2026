## 🖼️ Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)

> **Why learn CNNs for Graph ML?** > Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) share deep structural and conceptual similarities with CNNs. As you progress, you will frequently see references comparing spatial graph operations to grid-based convolutions (as well as RNNs and Transformers). Mastering CNNs now will make GNNs much easier to grasp later.

### 🎥 CNN Core Lectures

* **[But what is a Convolution? by 3Blue1Brown](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HGwBXDKFk9I)** *Watch this if the mathematical concept of a "convolution" feels unclear in the introductory lectures.*
